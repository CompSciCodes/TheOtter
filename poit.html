Computer Technician

In the Foundation of Cybersecurity course, you’ll find out what the IT Fundamentals exam is all about because each lesson covers a part of the exam. Review questions are included at the end of each lesson to help crystallize the information you learned and solidly prepare you to ace the exam. This course covers more than just the exam, though. It includes a foundation of IT knowledge that will prepare you for real jobs, not just to pass a test. After all, life is not a multiple-choice test with the answers clearly laid out in front of you!

A really cool thing about working in IT is that it’s constantly evolving, so there are always new things to learn and fresh challenges to master. Once you obtain your IT Fundamentals certification and discover that you’re interested in taking it further by getting into more complex topics (and making more money), the CompTIA A+ certification is definitely your next step.

~uCertify Course Description
